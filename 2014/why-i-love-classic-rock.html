Why I love Classic Rock.

Music for soul.

Posted by Anurag Aryan on December 21, 2014
Tagged with music.

The Beatles

From The Beatles I learned how to live in peace and harmony with other people

Led Zeppelin

From Led Zeppelin I learned what love actually means

Jimi Hendrix

From Jimi Hendrix I understood what happiness and beauty mean

Bob Dylan

From Bob Dylan I learned that it’s okay to change yourself with time and say what you want to say even when you’re expected to say different

Black Sabbath and Dio

From Black Sabbath and Dio I learned that I should appreciate sadness, because it makes us real

Metallica and Megadeth

From Metallic and Megadeth I understood the suffering of others and how I should do something about it.

Queen

And of course, from Queen I understood that hurting others will make me suffer too.

Nirvana

From Nirvana I understood that pleasure comes from me, so I should find things that I enjoy, not conform to others.

Deep Purple and Rainbow

and well,Deep Purple and Rainbow are one of thee things that I enjoy because they make real, genius music.

All this has now made me into the person I am today, and honestly, I appreciate it.
