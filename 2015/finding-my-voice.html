Finding my voice

And then there was a search within.

Posted by Anurag Aryan on July 1, 2015
Tagged with life, thoughts.

Raw Thoughts blog number two.

I’ve been on Quora and tried being intellectual, and on twitter trying to show my funny side, faked on facebook my real self, on reddit being the silent observer and within the stackoverflow community acting as know it all, nothing worked.

I’m not one of those who smoke half a quart of marijuana and a six-pack but I know one who does, I don’t study from day in and day out but I know one of that kind, I’m not courageous enough to drop out of college to pursue my dream but there have been people in my life who have dared to do so, I don’t go gyming daily, bulking up and gaining muscles but some people I like do, I can never be a Casanova with ladies but been with such personalities for long enough to learn all the moves. So my voice is a borrowed one, I may not be interesting enough for you but have enough stories to interest you. I tell tales of people you may know or maybe not, I don’t really care. After all life is too short to care for everyone.
