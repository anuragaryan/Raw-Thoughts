And then there was a post.

Posted by Anurag Aryan on April 4, 2015
Tagged with college.

Raw Thoughts blog number One.

I am not a writer nor your traditional a post a week blogger. Don’t expect anything from this blog or you’ll be disappointed. This is just going to be the dumping zone for my brain’s musing.

A few things I’ve learned in my travels through this crazy college life which is about to end.

One, A morning of awkwardness is better than a night of loneliness.

Two, You’ve your whole life to make sensible and mature decisions, this is not the time.

Three, GPA is not important, except during placements, approaching teachers for project, asking class nerds for favors, or to deserve respect from juniors.

Four, You’re never going to remember the 8am class in the morning. What you’re going to remember is the 9am screw up you did between the 8am & 10am class.

Five, Go talk to her asshole, she won’t kill you and anyway at this stage there’ll be no heartbreak that alcohol won’t fix.

Before I wrap this up, I want to ask why is this country of Engineers producing sub-par engineers working in a call centre?
